Past Events

Bombay Art Society Annual Exhibition

Author: Raja Ravi Varma

The Bombay Art Society Annual Exhibition was among the earliest platforms showcasing classic oil paintings in India. Under the influence of artists like Raja Ravi Varma, it brought together traditional techniques and rich color palettes to celebrate Indian cultural narratives. This event set a high benchmark for artistic excellence and innovation, inspiring generations of painters.

Calcutta Classic Oil Painting Salon

Author: Abanindranath Tagore

Organized by Abanindranath Tagore, the Calcutta Classic Oil Painting Salon was a landmark event that showcased the evolution of oil painting techniques during the Bengal Renaissance. The exhibition merged traditional artistry with emerging modern themes, drawing attention from art connoisseurs nationwide. Its influence helped redefine the visual language of Indian art.

Delhi Heritage Oil Showcase

Author: Nandalal Bose

The Delhi Heritage Oil Showcase celebrated the deep-rooted tradition of oil painting in India. With Nandalal Bose at the helm, the event featured works that narrated cultural and historical tales through classical techniques. It was widely regarded for reviving interest in indigenous art forms and promoting a nuanced understanding of Indian heritage.

Chennai Classic Canvas Expo

Author: S. S. Ramaswami (curator)

The Chennai Classic Canvas Expo provided a dedicated platform for South Indian oil painters to display their mastery. Organized by curator S. S. Ramaswami, the expo featured a collection of works that seamlessly blended traditional styles with contemporary nuances. It reinforced Chennai's reputation as a thriving center for cultural and artistic exchange.
